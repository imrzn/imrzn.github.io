To commemorate our most recent launch week (Aug 28th - Sep 1st, 2023), we had called upon our vibrant community to showcase their creativity, enthusiasm, and love for Xata. The challenge? Write a captivating blog post, create an engaging video, or simply spread the word about Xata. And you all delivered in style!

First and foremost, we want to give a heartfelt thank you to all our participants. Your dedication, creativity, and efforts truly amazed us. Each contribution is a testament to the incredible talent and passion within our community.

The anticipation has been building, and now, we're thrilled to reveal the winners of our content hackathon.
